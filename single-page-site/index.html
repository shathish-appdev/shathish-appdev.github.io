About Us

TechSV is an edutech firm based in Salem, India, that offers courses in robotics, programming, maths, science, and other subjects for school students in grades 5-9. Our mission is to inspire a love of learning and equip students with the skills they need to succeed in the 21st century.

We believe that learning should be fun and engaging, which is why we've designed our courses to be hands-on and interactive. Our team of experienced educators and industry professionals are passionate about sharing their knowledge and helping students achieve their full potential.
